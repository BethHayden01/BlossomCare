Best Rated Houseplant Products

When chosing a plant to introcude to your home, it is important to consider your environment. Houseplants, just like any other outdoor plants, each require different levels of sunlight or water depending on their bread. If these requirements are not met, the plant will be unlikely to survive. However, there are a number of products avaiable to provide houseplants with the best chance of survial.

Fertilizer

Fertilizing your houseplants is an important part of plant care. Fertilizers provide your plants with the important essential nutrients they need to thrive. No matter how perfect the conditons are in your home, houseplants always benefit from a little nutritence boost, just as you benefit from taking vitamins. There are a large amount of fertilizers avaliable on the market today, some of the best are shown in the image above.

Leaf cleaner

One of the most imporant organs on a plant are its leaves as this enables them to absorb sunlight. If these leaves become clogged with dirt the plant will struggle to access the sunlight it needs. In order to ensure your plants leaves are able to fully access this vital element to their survival, you can purchase a plant leaf cleaner such as the Leaf Shine shown in the image above.

Plant containers

It is important to ensure your houseplant is in the correct container otherwise your houseplant can become root-bound or waterlogged. There are many different types of plant containers avaliable on the market today such as the glass terrarium shown in the above image. Glass terrariums are perfect for small succulents or cacti as they keep in heat and provide complete access to sunlight.

Humidifyers

Certain species of houseplant require humid environments in order to thrive. If you do not live in a naturally humid environment you can provide your houseplant with this essential element through the use of a plant humidifyer. These devices work the same as a home humidifyer but on a smaller scale.
